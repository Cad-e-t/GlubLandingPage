Write Without Leaving

Don't plan. Don't research. Just write.

Stay in the Flow

Glubook AI lets you write without stopping to look up details, find the perfect word, or remember past scenes. Use {brackets} with smart tags to mark gaps and keep going.

  • Need a better word? He was {#word intense but quiet}
  • Forget her eye color? Her eyes were {#memory}
  • Unsure about nun attire? They wore {#research}
  • Glubook fills in the blanks when you're done, so you never lose momentum.

The Problem Glubook Solves

Writers lose their flow when they stop to:

  • Search Google for details
  • Scroll back to remember things
  • Find the perfect word
  • Plan before writing
  • Edit as they go

Glubook eliminates these interruptions, letting you finish whole chapters without leaving your story.

How It Works

Write freely and let Glubook handle the rest:

  1. Write your chapter, using {brackets} for anything you don't want to stop for.
  2. Click "Done Writing" - Glubook AI processes all {brackets} in context.
  3. Review suggestions for words, memories, or research, and accept or revise.
  4. Click "Remove Brackets" for a clean, polished chapter.

Stay in control while Glubook keeps your ideas flowing.
